DOCUMENT TYPE: Piping and Instrumentation Diagram (P&ID) - Text Representation
SYSTEM: Primary Reactor Cooling Loop
DIAGRAM ID: PID-402-REV-A

COMPONENTS AND TAGS:
1. PMP-102A (Primary Cooling Water Pump)
   - Type: Centrifugal Pump
   - Design Pressure: 150 PSI
   - Flow Rate: 120 GPM
   - Connected to: Pipe Segment L-01 (Suction) and L-02 (Discharge)

2. VLV-304 (Main Flow Control Valve)
   - Type: Pneumatic Control Valve
   - Fail State: Fail-Closed (FC)
   - Connected to: Pipe Segment L-02

3. HX-201 (Primary Heat Exchanger)
   - Type: Shell and Tube
   - Shell Side fluid: Cooling Water
   - Tube Side fluid: Reactor Product
   - Operating Temperature: 85°C

4. SENS-T45 (Temperature Transmitter)
   - Range: 0-150°C
   - Alarm High (AH): 95°C
   - Alarm High-High (AHH): 105°C (Triggers Emergency Shutdown)

PROCESS FLOW SUMMARY:
The centrifugal pump (PMP-102A) draws cooling water from the main reservoir and feeds it through the control valve (VLV-304). The water then enters the shell side of the heat exchanger (HX-201) to absorb heat from the reactor product. Temperature is continuously monitored by SENS-T45 at the discharge outlet.
