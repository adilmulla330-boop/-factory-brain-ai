TitanForge Industries - Company Overview & Details

Founded: 1985
Headquarters: Detroit, Michigan
Core Business: Heavy industrial manufacturing, precision metallurgy, and large-scale machining for aerospace, automotive, and energy sectors.

Facilities:
- Plant A (Detroit, MI): Primary casting and heavy machinery operation. ISO9001 and ISO45001 certified. Features 12 heavy duty CNC mills and 4 industrial blast furnaces.
- Plant B (Cleveland, OH): Specialized in precision gears, hydraulic systems, and compressor testing. Features a dedicated Quality Assurance lab and non-destructive testing (NDT) facility.
- Plant C (Dallas, TX): Assembly and logistics hub. Handles integration of motors, gearboxes, and control valves before final shipping.

Executive Leadership:
- CEO: Marcus Sterling
- VP of Engineering: Sarah Jenkins
- Chief Safety Officer: David Thorne

Strategic Goals (2026-2030):
1. Achieve zero unplanned downtime across all plants using predictive AI maintenance.
2. Reduce carbon footprint by 25% via optimized blast furnace scheduling.
3. Attain 100% compliance with OSHA and PESO regulations.
